Our Services

VIRTUAL ASSISTANT

Virtual assistants play a crucial role in providing remote administrative support to clients. They operate from home offices and handle various tasks. Here are some common responsibilities of virtual assistants: Scheduling Appointments: Virtual assistants manage calendars, arrange meetings, and schedule appointments for their clients. Phone Calls: They handle phone calls, answer inquiries, and relay messages. Travel Arrangements: Virtual assistants assist with travel planning, booking flights, accommodations, and organizing itineraries. Email Management: They handle email correspondence, filter messages, and prioritize important communications. Data Entry: Virtual assistants input data, maintain databases, and organize information. Social Media Management: They create and schedule social media posts, engage with followers, and monitor online presence. Research: Virtual assistants conduct market research, gather information, and compile reports. Administrative Tasks: These can include document preparation, filing, and other administrative duties.

CAREER COUNSELLING

Career counselling chatbots have become essential tools for both students and professionals seeking guidance. These bots help career counsellors manage their workload efficiently and provide better services to clients. Collecting Information: Chatbots gather relevant background details, interests, and aspirations from candidates. Screening Profiles: They assist in screening candidate resumes, saving time for counsellors. Counselling History: Chatbots maintain a record of conversations, preventing repetition and ensuring continuity.
